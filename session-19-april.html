Endzone

Horizontal endzone offence — can adjust into vertical if needed (isolate front of stack, cuts from the back)

Guidelines
  1. Side stack players stay disciplined and don't drift out to help
  2. Once we have started a swing, finish it
  3. ISO player — don't cut open as you clog the space for reset to cut upline

Offence Aims

  • Isolate cutters
  • Swing the disc
  • Can adjust into vertical endzone if needed

Defence Aims

  • Comms and heads-up D
